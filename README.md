A specialized prompt engineering framework for AI video models (Sora, Kling, Seedance) focused entirely on generating highly viral, comedic, and meme-worthy cat content. Master feline micro-expressions, internet-native camera styles (like fisheye and CCTV), and physical comedy.

AI video models require highly specific, visually grounded language to produce consistent results. This framework acts as your "Viral Meme Director," transforming funny ideas into structured prompt matrices by controlling:
*   **Cat Breeds & Traits** (e.g., Orange Tabby, Blep, Dilated Pupils)
